Parameter Explanations

  • P: Represents the level of phosphorus in the soil. It's crucial for root development and energy transfer in plants.
  • pH: Measures the acidity or alkalinity of the soil. Most crops prefer a pH range of 6 to 7.
  • OM: Stands for Organic Matter percentage. It indicates the amount of decomposed organic materials in the soil.
  • EC: Electrical Conductivity reflects the soil's ability to conduct electrical current, indicating the level of soluble salts.
